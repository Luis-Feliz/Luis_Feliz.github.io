Gaming Projects

Diagnosis Simulator

Made in Unity for an Internship with Saint Luke's University Hospital as a leader in a team of 6.

Made the animations and helped lead a team of 6 in the engineering, designing, and animating for a client as a means to give an educational VR experience to children and adults who are curious about certain conditions and how they affect the body.

One Way Ticket

Made in Unity for a 2D Game Development Course as the lead in a team of 4.

Lead the charge for the code in a majority of the game including the movement, scene transitioning, inventory, and enemy spawning code. Helped create Music, SFX, and animations for paralaxing the background trees alongside the train art.

SlimeHunter

Made in Unity for a 3D Game Development Course.

Although not fully complete, contains an inventory and combat system fully coded by me! Containing a turn-based battle system, inventory, and switching cameras inside and outside combat to see and play the games from different angles.

Animations and textures are not created by me, however, slime animation was.

GrindRunners

Made in Unity as a side project during the summer inspired by the mobile game craze of Subway Surfers.

Created obstacles that instantiate at random once the platforms reach a certain level. The game would progressively get faster the more points and coins you collect. The game is incomplete, however, the mechanics are fleshed out. Instantiation of platforms also helped in the creation of the paralaxing in OneWayTicket.

SmartBullet

Made in Unity for a game jam being run in my college.

Created movable blocks that become still when you begin to escape. The puzzle revolving around finding the easiest way to move from point A to point B without getting hit by the pellets that the blocks let out.
