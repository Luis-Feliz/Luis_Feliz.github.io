Bio

Luis's approach towards Game Development is characterised by his deep love and respect towards the medium. He has grown to pay attention to every single portion of the game design process from the intensive coding engines to the musical both in song and SFX. He prides himself to be a grand jack of all trades and believes that the best games don't come from just one thing done perfect but the combination off all mediums inside a game working in symphony with one another. Born in Dominican Republic and moving between there and New York, Luis grew up being surrounded with many different cultures and experances that has shaped his form of expression. In the midst of his growth, he found love in both games and stories, of which he was infatuated by how they can invoke strong and enaging emotions with their reader/player that have moved him and inspired him to create. He now yearns to create games that have moved and bring out emotions of others the same way they have done to him.
