About Me

______________________________________

Luis's approach toward Game Development is characterized by his deep love and respect for the medium. He has grown to pay attention to every single portion of the game design process from the intensive coding engines to the music both in song and SFX. He prides himself to be a grand jack of all trades and believes that the best games don't come from just one thing done perfectly but the combination of all mediums inside a game working in symphony with one another.

Born in the Dominican Republic and moving between there and New York, Luis grew up surrounded by many different cultures and experiences that have shaped his form of expression. Amid his growth, he found love in both games and stories, of which he was infatuated by how they can invoke strong and engaging emotions in their readers/players that have moved him and inspired him to create.

He now yearns to create games that have moved and bring out the emotions of others the same way they have done to him.

Recently, Luis has just graduated from Kutztown University of Pennsylvania as the very first Game Development in KU history and is eagerly preparing and applying to seek out employment to further his dream in the area of New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, New Hampshire, Boston, or Remote.
